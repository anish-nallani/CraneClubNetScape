Crane Club Timeline

1/16/24 - Crane Club created at Wayne State University
4/3/24 - 70 Cranes in boxes donated to CS Mott’s Inpatient Psychiatric Children’s Hospital
4/16/24 - www.craneclub.cc is created
5/16/24 - Crane Club summer meetings take place every Thursday
6/25/24 - Crane Club’s name is reserved
6/28/24 - 100 Cranes in boxes donated to CS Mott’s Inpatient Psychiatric Children’s Hospital
6/28/24 - Partnered with the Canton Public Library to host a public meeting
7/26/24 - Crane Club officially named a non-profit in the state of Michigan
8/3/24 - Partnered with 88.1, the Park, a local radio station to air a news story on Crane Club
8/6/24 - 260 Cranes donated to Ann Arbor Meals on Wheels
8/12/24 - Partnered with the Gifts of Arts program to create origami kits for adult inpatients
8/15/24 - Partnered with CupsNChai to promote mental health awareness in the desi community
9/02/24 - 620 cranes donated to the Children’s Hospital of Michigan
9/14/24 - 50 Cranes donated to Turner Senior Resource Center
9/29/24 - Partnered with the Canton Hindu Temple to promote mental health awareness
10/6/24 - Featured on NPR WDET 101.9 FM for donating 1000 cranes to Michigan hospitals
10/9/24 - Partnered with University of Michigan’s United Asian American Organizations
10/14/24 - 50 cranes donated to Silver Club Memory Programs
10/25/24 - 250 cranes donated to the Gifts of Arts program
11/14/24 - Collaborative art build with UAAO and TAHRIR Coalition
11/14/24 - 500 cranes donated to the Gifts of Arts program
11/15/24 - Crane-folding workshops hosted with Children’s Hospital of Michigan
12/1/24 - Month-long 2000 paper crane exhibit at Michigan Medicine
12/14/24 - 3-hour crane workshops at Med Inn, Family Center, and Lecture Hall
2/27/25 - 2-month crane art exhibit with Children’s Hospital of Detroit volunteer services
5/12/25 - Crane folding workshop with patients via Gifts of Arts
6/13/25 - 500 cranes donated + 2-hour workshop hosted with Gifts of Arts
7/27/25 - Crane workshop and dating violence seminar with CHASS Center youth group
