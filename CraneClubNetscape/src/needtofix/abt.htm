   About Crane Club

Crane Club is a non-profit organization dedicated to bringing hope and comfort to patients in Michigan hospitals by donating handcrafted paper cranes. We are committed to pushing mental health awareness through the meditative process of origami.

We meet every Friday from 5-7 PM in Student Center Room 009. Updates regarding meeting times, activities, etc. will be posted on our instagram page linked here.
